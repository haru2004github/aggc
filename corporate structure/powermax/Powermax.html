Control System

The PLC-5110 control system is used to automatically start and stop the generator via remote control signal. It is configurable in three positions: manual, auto, and stop.

  • Stop stop generator / failure indication
  • Manual Only green manual button to start generator
  • Auto Under working conditions when it is energized, automatically start generator if receiving remote control signal.

Auto Transfer Switch (ATS)

For emergency power in buildings, an Auto Transfer Switch (ATS) is essential. The ATS can automatically transfer load from main power to emergency power without operator intervention.

When the main power fails or voltage drops below 80% of normal voltage, the ATS will start the emergency generator, after a preset time of 0 to 10 seconds (adjustable). At rated speed, the ATS will transfer load to emergency power with available size ranging from 63A to 3150 A. There is a switch suitable for every project's need.

Key Features

  • Mechanically held contactor
  • 4 pole, 3 pole option
  • Rating range from 63 to 3150 Amps
  • NEMA 1, 3R, 4, 4X and 12 enclosures
