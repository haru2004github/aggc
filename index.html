ABOUT US

AGGC is a diversified industrial and engineering group formed through the integration of Aung Gyi Group of Companies and Alliance Global Group Company.

With operations across Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Singapore, Hong Kong, and the UAE, the Group maintains an expanding regional presence across Asia and the Middle East. It connects regional and international partners to deliver coordinated engineering, manufacturing, and project execution services.

The Group is organized into four main divisions: Electric & Energy, Machinery & Automobile, AGGC Industries, and EPC + Finance, which collectively support its engineering, production, and service capabilities across multiple sectors.
