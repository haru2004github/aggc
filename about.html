About AGGC

Discover the history, vision, and core values that drive Aung Gyi Group of Companies forward in Myanmar's industrial landscape.

Brief Introduction

Established in 2004, AGGC stands as one of the leading machinery companies in Myanmar.

We distribute the most reliable and cost-effective construction machinery and equipment.

We have our own professional engineering team and service facilities providing after-sales services and warranty supports.

We are looking forward to achieve long term business development based on customers' trust, reliability and supports.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable company in Global Machinery & Automobile Industries

Our Missions

  • To develop mutual benefits between customers and company
  • To sustain customers satisfaction by providing quality products and utmost services
  • To revere for the stakeholders' needs and wants
  • To apply TQM (Total Quality Management) system in practice

Core Values

  • Compliance with State policies
  • Being above the average living standard for all
  • Keeping the values of business ethics
  • Sincere relationship with stakeholders
  • Elevation of our integrity and reputation
