Brief Introduction

Established in 2004, AGGC stands as one of the leading machinery companies in Myanmar.

We distribute the most reliable and cost-effective construction machinery and equipment.

We have our own professional engineering team and service facilities providing after-sales services and warranty supports.

We are looking forward to achieve long term business development based on customers' trust, reliability and supports.

Our Corporate Ecosystem

Corporate Structure

Aung Gyi Group of Companies (AGGC) operates as a parent conglomerate driving growth across major industrial fields through three specialized corporate divisions.

Alliance Tractors

Heavy Machinery Platform

Alliance Tractors serves as the premier platform for heavy construction and mining machinery. It connects international brands with key markets across Southeast Asia and the Middle East, offering equipment sales, leasing, and comprehensive parts support.

UAE, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Hong Kong
LiuGong, JAC Motors, Zega, Truston, Roadmax, Topmax

Powermax Industries

Energy & Power Solutions

Powermax Industries specializes in providing one-stop power generation systems. From high-quality diesel generators and intelligent auto-start control panels (PLC) to industrial water pumps and custom ATS solutions, they power critical infrastructure and industries.

Singapore, Myanmar (Yangon)
Silent Generators, Open Sets, ATS, Control Panels, Pumps

Turbo Oil Corporation

Oil, Gas & Energy Resources

Turbo Oil Corporation is a dynamic energy company operating across upstream, midstream, and downstream sectors. It provides oil exploration rig services, logistical transport solutions, and premium lubricants including engine, hydraulic, and gear oils.

UAE (Dubai), Singapore, Myanmar
Turboil lubricants, Emarat, Seismic Surveys, Rig Supplies
